## Rule of Inference


| $p\to q\\ \neg q$ | $p\to q\\ q$ | $p\and q\\p$ | $p$      | $p\to q\\q\to r$ | $p\or q\\ q\to r\\ q\to s$ |
| ----------------- | ------------ | ------------ | -------- | ---------------- | -------------------------- |
| $\neg p$          | $p$          | $q$          | $p\or q$ | $p\to r$         | $r \or s$                  |
